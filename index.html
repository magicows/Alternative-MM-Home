WHAT WE DO

Project Management

We run projects in an intuitive yet efficient way without the financial overhead often associated with traditional project management. We deliver on time to budget while satisfying any external requirements such as audits.

Agile and Scrum

Our consultants are certified Scrummasters and experienced in successfully delivering projects using Agile methodologies. We can provide the leadership to assist your organisation in implementing Scrum. We are active in the community and bring our thought leadership and experience to your projects.

PRINCE2

We have the versatility of practicing traditional or agile project management methodologies and Magic Milestones consultants are all certified Prince2 Practitioners with extensive track records of delivering success using Prince2 in our specialist sectors.
